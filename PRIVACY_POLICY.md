# Privacy Policy for Inventory Manager by CATronics

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

CATronics ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Inventory Manager by CATronics mobile application ("App") handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using secure local storage:

- **Product Information**: Product names, categories, buying prices, selling prices, stock quantities, supplier names, and optional product images
- **Transaction Records**: Sales, purchases, and inventory removal records including dates, quantities, prices, and optional notes
- **App Settings**: Company name and other app configuration preferences

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts, camera (except when you choose to add product images), or other sensitive device features without your explicit permission
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT sell, share, or rent your information to third parties

## How We Use Your Information

All data entered into the App is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

- Manage your product inventory
- Track sales, purchases, and inventory changes
- Generate reports and invoices
- Calculate profits and stock values
- Display dashboard summaries

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption through AsyncStorage
- Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers
- You have full control over your data and can delete the App at any time to remove all stored information
- We recommend that you use your device's built-in security features (passcode, biometric authentication) to protect access to your device and the App

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

- We do NOT share your data with any third parties
- The App operates entirely offline and does not require internet connectivity to function
- No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used in the App

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- Access all data stored in the App at any time
- Modify or delete any products, transactions, or settings
- Permanently delete all data by uninstalling the App from your device
- Export data through the invoice/report generation features

## Children's Privacy

The App does not specifically target children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed for business inventory management purposes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as the App is installed. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data from your device.

## International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, this App complies with data protection regulations including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) by design, as no personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers.
